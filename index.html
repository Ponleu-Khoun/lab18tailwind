About Us

The Royal University of Phnom Penh is Cambodia's oldest and largest university, dedicated to providing quality education and fostering research opportunities.

Our Programs

Bachelor of Science

Explore various scientific disciplines and enhance your research skills.

This program covers biology, chemistry, physics, and mathematics, providing a comprehensive understanding of scientific principles and research methodologies. Students will engage in hands-on experiments, field studies, and collaborative projects that foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Graduates will be well-prepared for careers in research, healthcare, environmental science, and education. Additionally, the program emphasizes the importance of ethics in science, encouraging students to consider the broader implications of their work. With access to state-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities, students can explore their interests and contribute to meaningful scientific advancements.

Bachelor of Arts

Dive into humanities and social sciences for a deeper understanding of society.

This program emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and communication skills, preparing students for diverse career paths in arts and social sciences. Courses may include literature, history, philosophy, and sociology, allowing students to explore the human experience from multiple perspectives. Graduates often pursue careers in education, public policy, media, and non-profit organizations. The program also encourages community engagement and social responsibility, providing students with opportunities to apply their knowledge in real-world contexts. Through internships and volunteer work, students can develop a strong professional network and gain practical experience that complements their academic studies.

Information Technology

Gain practical skills in IT and prepare for a tech-driven world.

The IT program focuses on software development, networking, and cybersecurity, equipping students with the skills needed in the modern workforce. Students will learn to design, implement, and manage technology solutions that solve real-world problems. Opportunities for internships and hands-on projects ensure that graduates are job-ready and capable of adapting to the ever-changing tech landscape. The curriculum also includes emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, data analytics, and cloud computing, preparing students for future innovations. With a strong emphasis on teamwork and communication, students will develop the interpersonal skills necessary to thrive in collaborative environments.
